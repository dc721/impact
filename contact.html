Frequently Asked Questions

What is concierge medicine and how is it different from a regular doctor? +

Concierge medicine is a membership-based model where patients pay a retainer fee in exchange for direct, unlimited access to their physician. Unlike a traditional practice with thousands of patients, we strictly limit our panel size so each patient receives same-day appointments, direct physician communication, and thorough, unhurried care.

Does Impact Medical accept insurance? +

We operate outside of the traditional insurance model as a direct primary care and concierge practice. This allows us to focus entirely on your care rather than on insurance requirements. Some services may be billed to insurance; please contact our office for detailed information on fees and membership options.

How do I get started as a new patient? +

The best first step is to schedule a complimentary discovery call or consultation with our team. We'll discuss your health goals, walk you through our services and membership options, and determine which of our physicians is the best fit for your needs.

Do I need to be sick to benefit from your services? +

Absolutely not — in fact, most of our patients are healthy, high-performing individuals who want to stay that way. Our philosophy is proactive, not reactive. The earlier we identify risk factors and optimize your health, the better your long-term outcomes will be.

Is Impact Medical only for people in Heber City, Utah? +

Our clinic is based in Heber City, Utah, and we serve patients throughout the surrounding area. Some of our services, including certain consultations and follow-up care, may be available via telehealth. Contact us to learn what options are available for your location.
