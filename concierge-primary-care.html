What It Is

Medicine the Way It Should Be

At Impact Medical, primary care looks nothing like a rushed 10-minute visit. As a concierge practice, we strictly limit patient panels so each physician can deliver the time, attention, and depth of relationship that genuinely personalized care requires.

You'll have direct access to your doctor — by phone, text, or same-day appointment — and annual comprehensive evaluations that go far beyond standard wellness exams. This is care built around your life, not around insurance billing codes.

We offer two models: our premium Concierge Membership for patients who want unlimited, year-round access, and Direct Primary Care for those seeking affordable, transparent monthly care without the constraints of insurance.
