Advanced Cardiac Care

Protect Your Heart Before Symptoms Appear

Heart disease remains the leading cause of death — yet the vast majority of it is preventable when identified early. Standard cholesterol panels miss most of the real cardiovascular risk. Our advanced cardiometabolic assessment goes far deeper, identifying arterial disease, calcification, and inflammatory risk years before symptoms arise.

Led by Dr. Ryan Cooley — a board-certified Cardiac Electrophysiologist with over 25 years of clinical experience — our Cardiometabolic Wellness program combines state-of-the-art diagnostics with evidence-based interventions to protect your heart and metabolic health for the long term.

We offer two connected services: a comprehensive Cardiometabolic Assessment for risk evaluation, and an ongoing Arrhythmia & Atrial Fibrillation Management program for patients with known cardiac rhythm conditions.

Our Services

Two Connected Programs

01

Cardiometabolic Assessment

A thorough cardiovascular and metabolic risk evaluation that goes far beyond what standard medicine typically orders. We use advanced imaging, biomarker panels, and clinical assessment to build a complete picture of your cardiovascular and metabolic risk — then design a targeted intervention strategy.

Advanced lipid panel: LDL particle number, ApoB, Lp(a), oxidized LDL
Coronary artery calcium (CAC) scoring
Inflammatory cardiovascular risk markers (hsCRP, IL-6, homocysteine)
Insulin resistance and metabolic syndrome assessment
Vascular age and arterial stiffness evaluation
Personalized statin, supplement, and lifestyle intervention planning
Starting At $2,400
02

Arrhythmia & Atrial Fibrillation Management

For patients with known cardiac arrhythmias, including atrial fibrillation (AFib), Dr. Cooley provides specialized ongoing management — combining his electrophysiology expertise with a holistic, integrative approach to rhythm management and stroke prevention.

This program is designed for patients who want expert cardiologist-level oversight within a concierge-style model — with the time and attention that a busy academic EP lab simply cannot provide.

Comprehensive AFib evaluation and rhythm monitoring
Rate vs. rhythm control strategy planning
Anticoagulation management and stroke risk assessment
Lifestyle and metabolic drivers of arrhythmia
Coordination with electrophysiology procedures when needed
Ongoing monitoring and care plan updates
Program Lead

Dr. Ryan Cooley, MD

Board-certified Cardiac Electrophysiologist with 25+ years of clinical experience. Dr. Cooley spent the majority of his career at the highest levels of academic and interventional cardiology before joining Impact Medical to offer something the traditional cardiology system rarely allows: true, ongoing physician-patient relationships built on time, trust, and advanced preventive care.

His approach combines the depth of an electrophysiology specialist with the holistic philosophy of integrative medicine — addressing the root metabolic and lifestyle drivers of cardiac disease alongside the clinical treatment of arrhythmias and vascular risk.
