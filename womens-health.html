Women's Integrative Care

Whole-Person Care for Every Stage of Life

Women's hormonal health is complex, nuanced, and profoundly impactful on every aspect of wellbeing — from energy and mood to bone density and cardiovascular health. At Impact Medical, we treat the whole picture.

Whether you're navigating perimenopause, dealing with symptoms that have been dismissed by other providers, or simply want to feel more like yourself, our women's health team takes a thorough, science-based approach to restore balance and optimize vitality.

Our 3-month program begins with a comprehensive hormone and metabolic evaluation, followed by a personalized treatment plan built around your specific symptoms, labs, and goals — with close monitoring and physician support throughout.

Common Concerns

Symptoms We Address

✦ Hot flashes and night sweats
✦ Fatigue, low energy, or sleep disruption
✦ Mood changes, anxiety, or depression
✦ Brain fog or difficulty concentrating
✦ Decreased libido or vaginal dryness
✦ Weight gain, especially around the midsection
✦ Irregular or heavy periods
✦ Thyroid imbalance symptoms (cold sensitivity, hair loss)
✦ Feeling "off" even when tests come back "normal"

Hormone Evaluation & Optimization

Full female hormone panel

Perimenopause & Menopause Management

Symptom assessment and staging
HRT / BHRT treatment planning
Bone density and cardiovascular risk assessment
Non-hormonal symptom management options

Thyroid & Metabolic Health

The thyroid affects virtually every system in your body. We assess and optimize thyroid function as part of an integrated approach to energy, weight, mood, and longevity.

Complete thyroid panel (TSH, Free T3, Free T4, antibodies)
Hypothyroid and Hashimoto's management
Metabolic rate and weight optimization
