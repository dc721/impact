Our Services

Men's Health Services at Impact Medical

01

Hormone Evaluation & Optimization

We begin with a comprehensive male hormone panel — not just total testosterone, but a complete picture including free testosterone, DHEA-S, estradiol, cortisol, SHBG, thyroid hormones, and metabolic markers. Understanding your full hormonal landscape is the foundation for effective treatment.

Based on your results, symptoms, and goals, we design a personalized 3-month treatment protocol with regular monitoring, dose adjustments, and physician check-ins throughout.

Comprehensive male hormone panel
Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT) — injections, pellets, or topical
DHEA, pregnenolone, and adrenal support
Thyroid optimization
Ongoing monitoring and protocol refinement
02

Sexual Health

Sexual health is an essential component of overall wellness — and one that many men feel uncomfortable discussing with a traditional doctor. At Impact Medical, we create a direct, judgment-free environment to address these concerns with science-based, effective solutions.

Erectile dysfunction evaluation and treatment
Libido restoration and sexual performance optimization
Hormone-based and non-hormone treatment options
Vascular and metabolic contributors to sexual health
03

Hair Loss Treatment

Male pattern hair loss is closely tied to hormonal changes and genetic factors. We address the underlying hormonal environment as part of a broader optimization plan, with targeted treatment options to slow progression and promote hair regrowth.

Hormonal and nutritional assessment for hair loss drivers
DHT-blocking therapy and scalp support protocols
Integrated with overall hormone optimization plan
Progress monitoring and protocol adjustment
