Our Story

About Impact Medical

Our Vision

We believe that the future of medicine is personalized, proactive, and physician-led — a model where high-performing individuals and dedicated physicians can thrive together.

A premier integrative medical group based in Heber City, Utah, Impact Medical was founded to redefine what healthcare can look like. We combine the expertise of elite, multi-specialty physicians with a deeply personal, relationship-driven approach that prioritizes the whole person — not just the chief complaint.

We exist for two groups of people: patients who want more from their healthcare experience, and physicians who want more from their careers. Through meticulous diagnostics, advanced therapeutics, lifestyle optimization, and compassionate ongoing care, we enhance wellbeing at every stage of life.

Our physician-led model spans hormone optimization, cardiometabolic health, cognitive wellness, and longevity medicine — delivering truly comprehensive, proactive care for the high-performing individuals and families who deserve it.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

01

Patient First, Always

Every decision we make — from the tests we order to how we design our clinical environment — starts with one question: what is best for this patient? We measure our success by your outcomes, not by throughput.

02

Precision Over Protocol

We don't apply one-size-fits-all solutions. We invest deeply in understanding your biology, your goals, and your life — then build a care plan as unique as you are.

03

Proactive, Not Reactive

Traditional medicine waits for disease to present. We act years earlier — identifying risk, optimizing biomarkers, and intervening proactively so illness becomes the exception.

04

Transparency & Partnership

You will understand your labs, your risks, and your options. Our physicians are partners in your health — not gatekeepers. You will always have direct access to your care team.

05

Excellence Without Compromise

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in diagnostics, therapeutics, and patient experience. From our facilities to our follow-up protocols, we settle for nothing less than exceptional.

06

Physician Autonomy & Innovation

We built Impact Medical so physicians can practice medicine the right way — with the time, resources, and freedom to truly care for each patient and stay at the forefront of what modern medicine offers.
