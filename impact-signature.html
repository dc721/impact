Why It's Different

More Than a Checkup — It's a Health Blueprint

Standard annual physicals are designed around insurance billing, not your biology. They check a small subset of routine markers, spend 15 minutes with you, and send you home with little actionable insight.

The Impact Signature Assessment is built differently. We spend significant time with each patient — understanding your history, your concerns, your goals — and pair that conversation with one of the most comprehensive laboratory and diagnostic evaluations available in outpatient medicine.

You leave with a complete, personalized health report, clear priorities, and a physician team that can help you act on every finding.

✦ No rushed appointments. Your assessment spans 1–2 visits with adequate physician time.
✦ No vague results. Every lab is explained in plain language with clinical context.
✦ No generic recommendations. Your health plan is built specifically for you.
